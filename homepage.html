Written and illustrated by Hirohiko Araki, Jojo's Bizarre Adventure (JJBA) was first serialized in Shueisha's Shonen Jump.

The story follows the Joestar family line as they battle various fruity foes with supernatural powers. The anime is known for it's over-the-top characters and storylines, unique art style, and iconic music references, as well as its particularly annoying fanbase.

Divided into 9 parts, each part follows a different "Jojo" with completely different storylines, some characters making various appearances throughout.
