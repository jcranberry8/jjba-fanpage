The Joestar Family Tree

All of the main characters (Jojos) come from the Joestar family, a family which starts the show as a family of English nobility and goes through various moves and marriages, resulting in American, Japanese, and “Italian” Jojo’s. This is how you end up with last names like “Kujo”, “Higashikata”, and “Giovanna”.
